What is Qi?

Qi is the energy that enables life, the force that runs through our bodies, that compose. You and me, our hands and our hearts, the neighbor's cat, the tree that they just finished of prune, all this is constituted and moves with Qi. Diseases also originate and spread alleviate hereby. A close translation would be “vital energy”. We all know the famous symbol of yin and yang, this is its essential rule: movement and change. the health of animals, the seasons of the year, the phases of a person's life, everything flows like yin and he yang, in an eternal change that, when interrupted and falls into imbalance, sickens and leads to unfavorable extremes.

Since ancient times, Qi has been worked with creating techniques that stimulate this energy overwhelmed and stuck in the body, many times accumulated and repressed by duties and concerns.

The medicinal networks developed by ancient masters and teachers are woven with the cosmos, having an intuitive knowledge that modern sciences are now rediscovering, the relationship between body and universe, seasons of the year and emotions, food and flavors, the sounds of the imagination, it becomes every day more important to rescue.

“If we do not understand the metaphors present in nature and in humans, it will not be possible to avoid nor effectively treat the disease.”
