Create to heal

Embrace of the tree displays a hybrid and accessible space in search of an alternative way to cultivate the relationship we have with our body. A way out of sedentary lifestyle and everyday life; find through artistic practice a new way of life, the renewal of exhausted energy. Transform daily stress into a dance for the senses with meditations, massages, movements body and musical landscapes.

“People today are different. Internally, they are enslaved by their emotions and concerns. They work too hard in their working lives.” The Yellow Emperor founds one of the oldest medicines in human civilization and, in conversation with the doctor Qi Bo, this current relationship between health and the human being is raised. Until today, there are healing techniques that help us avoid energy blockages that they generate diseases, learning to enjoy the body instead of suffering it. We found that the relationship between emotions, body and habits constitute today a game that generates deficiencies instead of strengthening us. Applying ancestral and current techniques we can reappropriate the spaces in our life to enjoy the days more and find the tools to push the borders that define us so much. Perhaps the healing arts have a better chance of healing us than all the behavioral teaching and pharmacological tablets together!
